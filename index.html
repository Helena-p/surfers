Upcoming teen camp in hawaii

Come join our Teen hawaii & Adventure Camp for the two week surf experience of a lifetime! This adventure is for beginner to advanced surfers ages 14-18 who want to learn how to surf or improve their skills while being immersed in the culture of Hawaii. Come have the time of your life while surfing several of Hawii best breaks and exploring the Pahoa region hawaii style!

We have been offering camps here since 2005 as the tropical climate, year-round waves, and 80 degree water temperatures make it a go-to destination for all levels of surfers.

Pre-training camp:

Are you an beginner and never have stand on a surf-board before, but you still want to be a part in our bigger camps?

WB Surf Camp has the program and prep-camp for you! Let's us help you get ready in time for our bigger experiances.

Advanced traning weekend:

If you are an intermediate to advanced surfer looking to effectively improve your water and competitive skills, WB Surf Camp has the program and coach for you!
